Bengali: Bagdhara (Idioms)
1. Bagdhara are special expressions used in Bengali language.
2. They give deeper meaning beyond the literal words.
3. Students present common idioms with examples and meaning.
4. Helps improve creative writing and communication skills.
5. Enhances cultural language understanding.
EVS (Geography): Layers of Atmosphere
1. Atmosphere is divided into layers like Troposphere, Stratosphere, Mesosphere, Thermosphere, and Exosphere.
2. Each layer plays a vital role in protecting Earth.
3. Ozone layer in the Stratosphere absorbs harmful UV rays.
4. Weather changes occur in the Troposphere.
5. Students prepare charts and 3D models of atmospheric layers.

History: Revolt of 1857
1. The Revolt of 1857 is considered the first war of Indian independence.
2. Major leaders included Rani Lakshmi Bai, Mangal Pandey, Nana Saheb, and Tatya Tope.
3. Causes included political, economic, military, and religious factors.
4. The revolt challenged British rule and inspired nationalism.
5. Students create timelines, maps, and leader biographies.
Civics: Court System of India
1. India has a three-level judicial system: Supreme Court, High Courts & District Courts.
2. Supreme Court is the highest authority for justice.
3. Courts protect fundamental rights of citizens.
4. Students learn courtroom procedures and landmark cases.
5. Helps understand law, justice, and democracy.
