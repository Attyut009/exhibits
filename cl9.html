Class 9 Exhibits

Hiru Panda | Samarpit Kundu | Ushashree Hembram
Science: Chandrayaan III
1. Chandrayaan-III is India’s third lunar exploration mission by ISRO.
2. It landed successfully on 23 August 2023 near the Moon’s south pole.
3. It included the Vikram lander and Pragyan rover.
4. The mission proved India’s soft landing capability.
5. The rover studied lunar soil, temperature and minerals.
Bengali: About the Poets
1. Students study the lives of famous Bengali poets.
2. Poets like Tagore, Jibanananda and Sukumar Ray are highlighted.
3. Their writing styles and messages are discussed.
4. Students present recitation, biographies and analysis.
5. Helps improve literary understanding and appreciation.
Computer: Java Games
1. Students create interactive games using Java.
2. Concepts like loops, conditions, OOP and events are used.
3. Games include Tic-Tac-Toe, Snake, Brick Breaker etc.
4. Enhances logical thinking and debugging skills.
5. Teaches real-world programming and creativity.
History: Fundamental Rights
1. Fundamental Rights are guaranteed by the Indian Constitution.
2. They include equality, freedom, religion and education rights.
3. Protects citizens from injustice and discrimination.
4. Students study case studies and real-life applications.
5. Shows how rights strengthen democracy.
Physics: Hydraulic Lift & Rain Sensor
1. Hydraulic lift works using Pascal’s Law (pressure transmission).
2. Demonstrates force multiplication through liquids.
3. Rain sensor detects water using conductive or IR circuits.
4. Used in automatic wipers and irrigation systems.
5. Students build working models showing automation.
Chemistry: Atomic Structure
1. Explains atom composition—protons, neutrons and electrons.
2. Students learn Bohr’s and quantum models.
3. Electrons arranged in shells and subshells.
4. Basis of chemical bonding and reactions.
5. Includes models of isotopes, ions and orbitals.
Mathematics: Statistics
1. Statistics deals with collecting and interpreting data.
2. Students calculate mean, median and mode.
3. Data represented using bar graphs and histograms.
4. Helps identify trends and patterns.
5. Projects include surveys and chart making.
