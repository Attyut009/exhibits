Exhibition 2025

The Annual School Exhibition is a platform designed to showcase the creativity, talent, and conceptual understanding of students. It transforms classroom learning into real-world applications through models, experiments, research work, and innovative presentations. This event not only strengthens problem-solving and communication skills but also builds confidence as students interact with visitors, explain their ideas, and demonstrate their understanding with clarity and originality.
